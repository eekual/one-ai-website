03 — The System

Two things interpreted directly.
One product produced.

01

Desire

A guided digital interaction reads what the customer responds to — what shapes, styles, and qualities feel right for them. Not a quiz. Not a filter. A continuous learning signal that converges on the individual's actual preference.

Every scroll, every pause, every selection and non-selection contributes. The system does not ask the customer to know what they want. It learns it.

02

Body

A smartphone captures the geometry of the customer's body or foot — from a few guided images. No clinic. No specialist. No hardware beyond the phone already in their pocket.

Keypoint detection accuracy of 98%+, with scan-to-production-ready parameters in under 3 seconds. The body is read as a signal, not a size.

03

Production

Preference and body combine into a unique manufacturing brief — created exactly once, for exactly this item, for exactly this person. It goes directly to production.

Local, flexible manufacturing. Off-the-shelf equipment. A workspace of 30 square metres is enough to begin. The supply chain compresses. The product arrives.

06 — The Environmental Consequence

SELFWEAR does not manage waste
at the end. It prevents it at the beginning.

Environmental gain is not a separate initiative layered on top of a commercial system. It is the consequence of changing the production logic itself.

No overproduction

If products are sold before they are made, overproduction begins to shrink structurally — not because of better disposal, but because the inventory that would need disposing of was never created.

No fit-based returns

60–70% of returns happen because the product did not fit. SELFWEAR addresses this at the source — a product interpreted from the body does not need to be returned because it did not fit a standard size.

Local production

Production close to demand means less transport, less logistics burden, and less CO₂ per unit. A SELFWEAR production unit can operate in 30 square metres using off-the-shelf equipment — in the city where the customer lives.

supplanted by SELFWEAR."
