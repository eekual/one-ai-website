01 / The Customer

The Old Way
Doesn't Fit.

Go ahead. Click your way through the online shop for any brand. As you do, a system watches what you pick and rearranges the existing offer. The products never change. The inventory is fixed. The best the system can do is sort: put the most likely match closer. Sooner or later, if you're lucky, you'll find an item you love.

But the system was never built around you. It was built around stock. Every garment was made before you arrived — made for a version of you that doesn't quite exist. Clothes that almost fit. Colors that come close. Style that's a compromise.

40% of all clothing made is never sold — never worn. Burned, landfilled, destroyed.

This is the system as it stands. Enormous production. Enormous waste. And still, the customer leaves without the clothes that are truly theirs. SELFWEAR was built to change this — not incrementally, but completely.

02 / The Empathy Engine

Selfwear is manufactured
in the unseen world
of your emotions.

SELFWEAR's EMPATHY ENGINE transforms the buying experience. Which is why it is such a powerful statement of who you are.

What you see on your screen is not selected from a fixed inventory. It's generated — uniquely for you. Selfwear generates a continuous stream of virtual designs, created by your favorite brand, as prompts. With each prompt an altered visual appears on your screen. You manipulate each, rejecting or accepting what you see in real time, altering the shape, style and colors.

Every scroll, every pause, even every non-selection shapes a flow that converges toward the clothes that are you. But Selfwear does more than just register each choice you click on. It gathers all the signals you unconsciously provide while choosing. The Empathy Engine synthesizes a shifting flow of parameters that is built on your behavior.

Think of it like a dating app — except you are searching for the clothes that match your identity, one desirable trait at a time. The flow converges toward a point in virtual space, begetting the clothes that are you.

"Not even tailor-made is this individual, this personal."

Only when you summon your clothes or footwear do they come into existence.

04 / The Environment

The clothes that love you,
also love the environment.

10% of global CO₂ emissions from the clothing industry
40% of clothing made is never sold, never worn
25–30% of all e-commerce clothing items are returned

Think about this: 10% of CO₂ emissions are generated by the clothing industry. Massive amounts of energy drive huge amounts of unnecessary production in places far, far away from the customer. Why unnecessary? Because 40% of clothing made is never sold, never worn. It's burned, landfilled, destroyed.

Legislation is demanding change. Starting July 19, 2026, the EU has passed a law making every brand legally responsible for disposing of any unsold stocks. The industry knows this. They just don't know what to do about it.

SELFWEAR makes this obligation irrelevant. With SELFWEAR the days of clothing waste are over. There are no stocks. Every item is made on demand — for a single person. Each item is sold before it's made. Or it doesn't get made.

No Returns

60–70% of returns happen because items don't fit. SELFWEAR eliminates this — millions of pixels from your smartphone generate a fit for the individual geometry of each customer's body, embracing it with precision.

Local Manufacturing

We make prototype sneakers in a workspace of 30 square metres with off-the-shelf machines costing €50,000. So brands no longer have to manufacture half-a-world away. The supply chain disappears, supplanted by SELFWEAR.

Zero Overproduction

Clothing no longer needs to be made first and then pushed through the sales machine. Every item is sold before it's made. The supply chain disappears, supplanted by SELFWEAR.
