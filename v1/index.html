02 / Preference Engine

The product emerges
through live preference.

ONE helps brands move beyond sorting fixed inventory. The system interprets preference in real time — shape, colour, proportion, emphasis — and narrows toward the right product direction in about five clicks.

Not from a catalog

What the customer sees is not selected from fixed inventory. The right product direction emerges through live interaction — uniquely, every time.

Every signal counts

Every scroll, every pause, every non-selection contributes. The system does not ask customers to know what they want. It learns it from their behaviour.

Guided live choice

The customer responds to evolving product prompts in real time. Each choice narrows the product direction without relying on fixed stock.

"Not even tailor-made is this individual, this personal."

SELFWEAR generates a continuous stream of virtual designs, created by the brand, as prompts. With each prompt an altered visual appears. Shape, style, and colour shift in response to what the customer accepts or rejects in real time.

But ONE does more than register each click. It gathers all the signals provided unconsciously while choosing — building a shifting flow of parameters from behaviour. That flow converges toward a specific product direction for this person.

Only when the customer confirms do those parameters go to production.

Stage 1 — Available now

A custom insole offered alongside existing footwear. Interpreted from a smartphone image of the customer's foot.

Zero inventory risk. No sizing. No scanning workflow. No changes to the existing shoe. ~58% incremental gross margin on the upgrade.

Light Integration

A personalised midsole or fit component integrated into the existing product. Partial personalisation without redesigning the full upper.

Same shoe, better fit. The brand gains premium pricing and richer first-party body data.

A complete personalised product produced on demand, near the customer. Sell-first. Zero inventory. Every product made for one person.

10% of CO₂ emissions are generated by the clothing industry. Massive amounts of energy drive unnecessary production in places far from the customer — unnecessary because 40% of clothing made is never sold, never worn. It is burned, landfilled, destroyed.

Legislation is accelerating the pressure. Starting July 2026, EU law holds every brand legally responsible for disposing of unsold stocks through a transparent, environmentally accountable process.

SELFWEAR changes the logic that creates waste.

When products are sold before they are made, overproduction begins to disappear at the source. SELFWEAR does not manage excess at the end. It reduces excess at the beginning.

Fit Returns

Fit-related returns are a major margin and waste problem. By interpreting the individual directly, ONE helps brands reduce mismatch at the source — not manage it after the fact.

Local Manufacturing

As production moves closer to demand, transport burden can shrink and the supply chain can become far lighter and more responsive. A SELFWEAR unit can operate in 30 square metres with off-the-shelf machines.

Made on Demand

Clothing no longer needs to be made first and then pushed through the sales machine. When every item is sold before it is made, the structural incentive for overproduction disappears.
