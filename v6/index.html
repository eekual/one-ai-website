The Environment

The system as it exists cannot sustain itself.

10% of global CO2 emissions come from fashion.
40% of all clothing is never sold.

Not because of failure.
But because of design.

The system requires overproduction
to function at all.

It produces before it knows.
It guesses before it learns.

And what is not sold
is erased.

Burned.
Buried.
Discarded.

This is not waste as an accident.
This is waste as a requirement.

Regulation now begins to respond.
Responsibility for unsold goods is shifting back to brands.

But regulation does not solve the root.

SELFWEAR does.

Because it removes the condition that creates waste.

Nothing is produced before it is wanted.

Every item is:
Sold first.
Then made.
Or never made.

Returns follow the same logic.

Fit is guessed.
So fit fails.

25-30% of all fashion e-commerce is returned.
Most of it due to fit.

SELFWEAR does not estimate fit.
It constructs it.

From the body itself.

The result is not optimization of the supply chain.
It is its disappearance.

Production moves to the point of demand.
The loop collapses.

What remains is direct:
desire → creation → delivery
